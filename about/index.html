I'm currently a freelance laissez-faire designer, a developer you shouldn't trust, an architect with a tech stack messier than a first LEGO set, a product designer whose designs have gone unnoticed, an operations guy with triple-digit views, and a blockchain developer who's experienced a few too many "Liquidation!" moments.

Though I often wear the hat of a Security amateur, my days aren't filled with the monotony of NetworkStreams. Instead, I craft applications and protocols in the vast and intriguing realm of cybersecurity. So, let's ditch the "Vulnerability = ALL" mindset, shall we?
